Porsche

The future is here

The Porsche Taycan embodies the future of automobiles with its groundbreaking electric technologies. This vehicle, a symbol of innovation, combines Porsche's legendary performance with fully electric propulsion, redefining the standards of the automotive industry.
With its ultra-efficient electric motors, the Taycan delivers lightning-fast acceleration while being environmentally friendly. Its high-capacity battery offers impressive range, while energy recovery systems maximize every kilometer traveled. Charging times are significantly reduced thanks to fast-charging technologies, making the Taycan as practical as it is electrifying.
Additionally, its advanced driver assistance systems, intuitive digital interface, and avant-garde design place the Porsche Taycan at the forefront of change, making electrification an exciting and accessible experience. The future of mobility is here, and it is both electric and thrilling.
Fully electric propulsion: The Porsche Taycan is a 100% electric model, designed to combine iconic Porsche performance with green technology.
Blazing Acceleration: Taycan models offer impressive acceleration. The Taycan Turbo S can go from 0 to 100 km/h in 2.8 seconds.
High capacity battery and range: The Taycan is equipped with a large capacity battery, up to 93.4 kWh, which allows a range of up to around 450 km depending on the WLTP cycle.
Energy recovery systems: It is equipped with an energy regeneration system during braking, which allows up to 90% of kinetic energy to be recovered.
Fast charging: The Taycan can recharge very quickly thanks to 800 volt technology, allowing it to add 100 km of range in 5 minutes of charging with fast charging stations.
Advanced technologies: The Taycan is equipped with numerous driver assistance systems and a modern digital interface with touch screens.
